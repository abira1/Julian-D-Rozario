🚨 TROUBLESHOOTING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Issue: Database connection failed
Fix: Check credentials in api/config.php, verify database exists

Issue: 404 on /api/ routes
Fix: Verify .htaccess uploaded correctly

Issue: Blank homepage
Fix: Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+R), verify index.html exists

Issue: Admin login fails
Fix: Check admin email in config.php AUTHORIZED_ADMIN_EMAILS

Issue: SSL not working
Fix: Wait 10 minutes, clear cache, check hPanel SSL status

For detailed troubleshooting, see DEPLOYMENT_GUIDE.md
